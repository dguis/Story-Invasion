Story Invasion:

Story Invasion is a game based off of all of the books read by D-Multi's CEO in the past year. Featuring Brandon Mull's Five Kingdoms and Fablehaven series', The True Confessions of Charlotte Doyle, and Richard Paul Evans' Michael Vey. Explore the realm of stories, mixing characters from different series and worlds. Unlock new characters to use their powers or talents, or create your own character! Unlock new powers and abilities for your character by completing quests throughout each magical world. Coming Soon, June 29th 2018.
